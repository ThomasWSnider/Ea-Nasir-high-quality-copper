Meet the Owner

Ea-Nasir was a copper merchant living in the City of Ur (near the Persian Gulf in modern day Iraq) at or around 1750 BC during the zenith of The Bronze Age. When what is thought to be Ea-Nasir's dwelling was excavated several clay tablets were found. These tablets were complaint letters from many Ea-Nasir's customers. The most famous of which is a tablet from a man named Nanni. The tablet became popular in an internet meme in 2017.
